Hi! My name is Wengsing Wong. I’m currently an incoming senior at Baruch College majoring in Computer Information Systems (General Track) and minoring in Mathematics. I previously completed courses in object-oriented programming and C++ (CIS 3100), database management systems (CIS 3400), and Python (MTH 3300). In Spring 2020, I completed courses in cybersecurity (CIS 3550), web design (CIS 3630), and fundamental algorithms (MTH 4320). In addition to my classes, I worked part-time in the Baruch Computing and Technology Center as a Desktop Management Support Assistant, where I learned more about computers and how to troubleshoot certain issues.

I also serve as the Social Media Chair of The Grid, Baruch’s only club dedicated to tabletop gaming. As Social Media Chair, it is my responsibility to manage the club’s Facebook and Instagram accounts and promote our various events throughout the year, such as RPS Tournament, Werewolves, and Big Game Day.

In my free time, I like to read fantasy novels, write stories, compose poetry, doodle comics, and photograph nature. I also enjoy playing all kinds of games (e.g. video games, tabletop games, computer games, etc.) and solving crossword puzzles, sudoku, and nonograms.
